# Portfolio Entry #4: Conditional 2 or 3 Story

## Story Chain 1 (Second Conditionals)
**Prompt:** If I had had one year off work...

If I **had had** one year off work, I'd **have traveled** to Mount Everest.
If I **hadn't traveled** to Mount Everest, I'd probably **have stayed** in Zurich and focused on my IT projects instead.

## Story Chain 2 (Third Conditionals)
**Prompt:** If I hadn't been feeling so terrible...

If I **hadn't been feeling** so adventurous, I **wouldn't have climbed** the mountain.  
If I **hadn't climbed** the mountain, I **would have missed** the stunning view from the summit.
